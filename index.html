Mitigation of Black Hole Attack in NoC

Designed and implemented a trust-based routing mechanism to identify and mitigate Black Hole attacks in Network-on-Chip (NoC) simulations. The solution involves maintaining a trust score for each router by analyzing packet delivery acknowledgments, enabling dynamic avoidance of malicious routers. This improves the security and reliability of on-chip communication by ensuring packets are routed through trusted paths, even in the presence of internal threats.
